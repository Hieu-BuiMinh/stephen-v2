## A Beautiful Escape to Bình Hưng Island - Our Unforgettable Trip in May 2025

_11-12 May 2025_

There are trips that simply become memories, and there are trips that stay with you for a lifetime. Our journey to **Bình Hưng Island** on **11-12 May 2025** was definitely one of those unforgettable experiences.

We were a team of **seven friends**, all excited for a short getaway after weeks of busy work and daily routines. What we didn't expect was that this island would leave such a deep impression on us—not only because of its breathtaking scenery, but also because of the laughter, friendship, and beautiful moments we shared together.

---

## Milestone 1: A Perfect Morning Welcome

Our trip started early in the morning as we arrived at Bình Hưng Island. The island welcomed us with **bright sunshine, crystal-clear turquoise water, and a peaceful morning atmosphere** that instantly made us forget all the tiredness from the journey.

At this time of year, the sea is unbelievably clear. The sunlight reflected on the water, creating sparkling waves that looked almost unreal.

What impressed us the most was how lively the island already was from **5:00 AM, or even earlier**. Fishing boats were already moving back and forth, local people had started their day, and the whole island was quietly bustling with life.

It felt like stepping into a completely different world—simple, peaceful, and incredibly beautiful.

## Milestone 2: The First Canoe Ride to Đảo Yến

After checking into our room and taking a short rest, we were taken by **canoe** to visit **Đảo Yến (Hang Tàu)**.

This was honestly one of the highlights of the trip.

The place is also known as **Hang Tàu**, because during stormy seasons, ships often come here to take shelter from the waves.

Inside the cave area, the water is naturally about **15 meters deep**, yet it is so clear that we could still see the bottom.
It was absolutely mesmerizing.

The surrounding limestone cliffs rose dramatically from the sea, untouched and wild. Everything still felt incredibly natural and raw, almost as if time had stopped here.

This was the first moment when I truly felt how beautiful nature in Vietnam really is.

The rocky mountains, the endless blue sea, the warm sunlight—all of it came together like a perfect painting.

Even though the sun was quite strong, we still made sure to take plenty of photos to capture the moment.

## Milestone 3: Fun at Bãi Cây Me

After visiting Đảo Yến, the boat owner took us to **Bãi Cây Me** for swimming.

This beach was simply stunning.

The water was cool and refreshing, the sea breeze smelled amazing, and the waves were gentle enough for us to enjoy playing in the water for hours.

Everyone was laughing, splashing water, and enjoying the moment like kids again.

One of the funniest moments happened here.

At one point, one of my teammates had pushed me underwater earlier and I accidentally swallowed a bit of seawater. Later, I “got my revenge” during our playful wrestling in the water and made him swallow some seawater too.

I was laughing so hard in that photo—it was probably one of the funniest pictures from the entire trip.

We also took a beautiful group photo together by the beach, with the clear sea behind us.

Those photos now feel priceless.

## Milestone 4: Seafood Lunch on the Floating House

After swimming, we returned to a **floating seafood house on the sea** for lunch.

And honestly—this meal deserves its own chapter.

Fresh seafood covered the entire table:
**clams, snails, fish, shrimp, and many other dishes freshly caught from the ocean.**

But the absolute star of the meal was the **sea urchin (nhum biển)**.

It was incredibly rich, creamy, and naturally sweet.

Apparently, this season is the best time for sea urchin, and after tasting it, I completely understood why.

It was easily one of the best seafood dishes I have ever had.

After lunch, we returned to our room and took a good afternoon rest, saving our energy for another exciting day ahead.

## Milestone 5: A Cozy Morning with Bánh Căn

The next morning started beautifully.

The weather was slightly chilly, with a gentle sea breeze drifting through the island.

For breakfast, we had **Bánh căn**, one of the local specialties.

Hot bánh căn in the cool morning weather was simply perfect.

The peaceful sight of boats floating slowly in the distance made the morning feel almost poetic.

Quiet, dreamy, yet still full of life.

It was one of those simple moments that somehow become unforgettable.

---

## Milestone 6: The Stunning Bãi Đá Trứng

After breakfast, a car took us to **Bãi Đá Trứng**.

This place was absolutely incredible.

The beach remains almost untouched by human hands, preserving its wild and natural beauty.

What makes it special is the countless smooth round stones that look like giant eggs scattered along the shore.

To this day, I still wonder how nature managed to create something so unique.

Of course, our whole team couldn't resist taking more photos and group shots here.

Every corner was picture-perfect.

## Milestone 7: Farewell to the Island and a Stop at Sheep Hill

After visiting Bãi Đá Trứng, it was finally time to say goodbye to Bình Hưng Island.

But the trip was not over yet.

Our tour guide then took us to **Đồi Cừu Suối Tiên**.

This place was such a fun surprise.

The sheep were adorable, friendly, and surprisingly affectionate.

They came close to us, making it easy to take selfies and funny team photos.

It added such a joyful and playful ending to the trip.

## Milestone 8: Vineyard and Local Specialties

Our final stop was a **grape garden in Ninh Thuận**.

We got to taste fresh grapes, grape wine, and local snacks such as dried fruits, cakes, and traditional sweets.

Everything was delicious.

Of course, I couldn't leave without buying some gifts for my family.

It felt like bringing a little piece of the journey back home.

---

## Ending: Goodbye Bình Hưng, Until We Meet Again

As the trip came to an end, I realized this journey was much more than just sightseeing.

It was about friendship.
It was about laughter.
It was about collecting beautiful memories with people who matter.

Bình Hưng gave us sunshine, crystal-clear water, amazing food, and unforgettable moments.

But more importantly, it gave us memories that we will continue to smile about for years.

Goodbye, Bình Hưng.
Thank you for everything.

Until we meet again.
