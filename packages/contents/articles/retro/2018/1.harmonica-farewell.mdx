Looking back at the summer of 2018, this moment remains etched in my memory. It was the first time I ever mustered the courage to play the harmonica in front of my entire class.

We were all standing on the threshold of a new chapter, preparing to head off to university and chase our dreams in different cities. This simple performance was my way of saying goodbye—and thank you—to the friends who had been by my side throughout high school. It wasn't a perfect performance, but it was heart-felt.
